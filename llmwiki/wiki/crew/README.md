# Crew

This folder contains knowledge related to human factors, crew performance, and wellbeing in isolated, confined, and extreme (ICE) environments.

Topics include:
- Fatigue and cognitive performance
- Team dynamics and conflict
- Sleep, nutrition, and circadian factors
- Behavioral indicators and early warning signs of crew degradation

This knowledge is critical because in small crews operating far from Earth, the human element is often the most fragile and least monitored part of the system.
